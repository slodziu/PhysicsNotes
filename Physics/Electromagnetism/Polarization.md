$$\vec{P}=\frac{\vec p}{V}$$
- [[Dipole Moment]] per unit volume

**Potential from a polarized object**:
$$V(r)=\frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\iint\frac{\sigma_b}{r}dA+\frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\iiint\frac{\rho_b}{r}d\tau'$$
with $\sigma_b = \vec{P}\cdot \hat n$ normal to surface and $\rho_b = -\mathbf{\nabla}\cdot \vec P$ 
- These are bound surface charge density and bound volume charge density
